SWP FROM TWO SCHEMES – SWP from one scheme is very common but may not work well when the markets are falling or volatile for a good number of months. What is the solution? You can invest in two schemes – One Liquid Fund and the other an Equity Fund or Hybrid Aggressive Fund. You can draw SWP from the liquid fund when the market is not doing well and when the markets are doing fine, draw from the Equity/ Hybrid Aggressive Fund.
